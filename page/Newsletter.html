    A newsletter is an email sent to people who signed up for it, which gives updates on specific topics.​ It encourages people to check the website more and stay interested and informed about the latest updates in the website.​

    By signing up, users will receive emails notifying them about new store openings in other cities or countries. Additionally, they will be alerted if the website experiences any downtime and informed about when it will be back online.

    ​When users sign up for a newsletter, they’ll receive exclusive deals and promo codes directly in their inbox. This ensures they are always informed about upcoming sales and discounts, giving them early access to products and special offers.
