    Introduction​

    Chocolate is a famous snack product all around the world. It was originally made with coco beans, to make the milk chocolate with a sweet taste by adding sugar. Ever since milk chocolate was made it has been sold all around the supermarkets and in shopping centers for people to purchase. However, chocolate is rich in carbohydrates, it has many health benefits and is a suitable source of quick energy. Although there are hazards of chocolate where it contains certain amounts of caffeine and theobromine which is highly risky and toxic for dogs. Most of the chocolate was made in some European countries such as in Germany, Belgium, Italy and Poland.​
